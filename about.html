About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Excellent Guest Hotel, located in the vibrant town of Wanguru, Kirinyaga County. We established this hotel with a simple mission: to provide a sanctuary of comfort, elegance, and exceptional service to locals and travelers alike.

Whether you are visiting for business, a family vacation, or just passing through, we strive to make your stay memorable. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that every guest feels at home, offering warm hospitality that reflects the spirit of Kirinyaga.

Why Choose Us?

  • Prime Location: Conveniently located in Wanguru, accessible and close to local amenities.
  • Comfort & Luxury: Modern rooms designed for relaxation and productivity.
  • Culinary Delight: Our restaurant serves the finest local and continental dishes.
